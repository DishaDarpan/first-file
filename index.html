What is Yoga?

'Samatvam Yogah Ucyate' means maintaining the state of mental equilibrium is Yoga. Yoga is a journey both outwards and inwards. Walking on this path, helps you understand your body, mind and soul. Yoga is an experience and becomes the way of your life with the experience you gain. It is not only a ‘Way of Life’, but a discipline in itself. So it has to be followed daily in order to avail its benefits.

Benefits of Yoga

  • Yoga improves strength, balance and flexibility.
  • Yoga helps with back pain relief.
  • Yoga can ease arthritis symptoms.
  • Yoga benefits heart health.
  • Yoga relaxes you, to help you sleep better.
  • Yoga can mean more energy and brighter moods.
  • Yoga helps you manage stress.
